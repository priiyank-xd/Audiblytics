Direction D

Single-Column Focus

Both rails removed. Day-rail compresses to a thin top strip. Everything in one centered column. Stats demoted to a thin footer row at the bottom of the page. Closest to iA Writer or a focused-reading app. Most distraction-free; least ai-2027-like in shape (though identical in palette/type).
